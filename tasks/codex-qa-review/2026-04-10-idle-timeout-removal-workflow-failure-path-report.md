- Medium: password reset creates a new custom app session and then immediately signs Supabase out in [auth.ts#L311](/Users/peterpitcher/Cursor/BARONS-BaronsHub/src/actions/auth.ts#L311) and [auth.ts#L348](/Users/peterpitcher/Cursor/BARONS-BaronsHub/src/actions/auth.ts#L348).
