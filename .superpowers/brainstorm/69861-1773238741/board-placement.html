Where do inspiration items live on the planning board?

The board already has 4 time buckets with planning items and event overlays. Where should inspiration items appear?

A

Inline in buckets — visually distinct cards

Inspiration items appear in the same 0–30 / 31–60 / 61–90 / 90+ day buckets as planning items, but with a distinct visual style (e.g. dashed border, sparkle icon). They feel like suggestions sitting alongside your actual plans.

31–60 days bucket
📋 Quiz Night – 14 Apr planning item
✨ Father's Day – 15 Jun inspiration Convert · Hide
📋 Staff Training Day – 20 Apr planning item
B

Separate "Inspiration" tab on the board

A new tab alongside the existing Board / Calendar / List views. When you switch to Inspiration, you see only the suggested events grouped by time bucket — a focused triage view. The main board stays clean.

C

Collapsible section at the top of each bucket

Within each time bucket, inspiration items appear in a collapsible "Upcoming occasions" section above the planning items. Collapsed by default to keep the board clean; expanding reveals the suggestions for that window.
